How to Play

  • After the game starts, Simon displays a sequence of colored buttons and corresponding sounds.

  • The player must repeat the sequence exactly as shown by pressing the buttons in the same order.

  • If the player correctly repeats the sequence, the game levels up, adding a new color to the sequence.

  • Each time the player advances a level, the sequence becomes longer.

  • If the player makes a mistake, the game ends, and they have the option to restart from the beginning to try again.

  • To restart, the player must begin again from the first level with the original sequence.

  • The goal is to see how many levels the player can complete without making a mistake.
